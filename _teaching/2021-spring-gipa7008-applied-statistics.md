I served as a **teaching assistant** for this graduate-level statistics course.

**Level:** Graduate  
**Role:** Teaching Assistant  
**Semester:** Spring 2021  

### Course description
The course covers core quantitative methods for social science research, including:

- Descriptive statistics and data visualization  
- Linear regression and model diagnostics  
- Causal inference basics and research design  

### Teaching responsibilities
- Held weekly office hours and review sessions  
- Assisted students with statistical software and replication exercises  
- Graded problem sets and exams  
- Provided feedback on empirical research projects
